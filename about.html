About

Richmond Custom Woodwork is the culmination of years of learning, practice, and hard work. My first experience with carpentry was helping a Class A contractor flip houses. I immediately fell in love with it, from the smell of sawdust to the feeling of a blade cutting through a 2x4. From there I worked for and with various other carpenters.

Eventually, I started working for a large scale, commercial millwork company. It was there that I developed a passion for the small details of finish carpentry such as cabinetry and trim work. After developing my skills there I was offered a position as a lead carpenter and project manager. I thrived being able to make design suggestions and having a more hands-on role with the clients. We are always looking for new projects no matter how big or small and can't wait to discuss the details with you.
